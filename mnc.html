🎯 CORE TARGETS — Ultimate Goals
UPSC CSE 🌟🌟🌟🌟🌟 Main Target · Final Yr or Post-Grad

Eligibility: Graduation complete (B.Tech any degree) & Age 21-32 years.

Timeline: Final Year Prelims or post-graduation.

Syllabus: Prelims: GS (Polity, History, Geo, Econ, Env) + CSAT Aptitude. Mains: Essay, 4 GS Papers, Languages + Maths Optional (Paper 1 & 2).

Vectors: GS & Lang: 3-hour cycle (Laxmikanth, Spectrum, Vivek Singh, Tarun Grover). Maths: 80% IISc Core overlap; balance via PYQs. DSA/Coding: Apna College.

GATE CS 🌟🌟🌟🌟 Main Tech Target · 3rd/4th Yr Feb

Eligibility: 3rd or 4th Year B.Tech any branch (MnC explicitly allowed).

Timeline: 3rd Year Feb (first shot) + 4th Year Feb (final score mapping).

Syllabus: Discrete Mathematics, Linear Algebra, Calculus, Programming & DSA, Digital Logic, Computer Architecture, OS, DBMS, Compiler Design, Networks, Aptitude.

Vectors: Core CS Theory: Standard books, NPTEL, GATE papers. Maths: IISc B.Tech roadmap + Manoj Chauhan.

GATE DA 🌟🌟🌟🌟 Main Data Target · 3rd/4th Yr Feb

Eligibility: 3rd/4th Year B.Tech (Perfect match for MnC).

Timeline: 3rd Year Feb (simultaneous with CS or standalone).

Syllabus: Probability & Statistics (Heavy), Linear Algebra, Calculus & Optimization, Python/C++, DSA, ML, AI.

Vectors: Maths: Manoj Chauhan + IISc curriculum. Lectures: SATHEE Portal + NPTEL.

🔬 ELITE MATH & STATS RESEARCH SPACES
IIT JAM (MA) 🌟🌟🌟🌟 · 3rd/4th Yr Feb

Eligibility: B.Tech/Bachelor's with Mathematics core.
Syllabus: Real Analysis, Multivariable Calculus, Linear Algebra, Group Theory, ODE. (50% smaller than IISc scope).
Vectors: Manoj Chauhan core modules + IISc textbooks. Practice past 10yr JAM books.

IIT JAM (MS) 🌟🌟🌟🌟 · 3rd/4th Yr Feb

Eligibility: B.Tech/Bachelor's with stats/math modules.
Syllabus: Calculus, Matrix Ops, Probability, Distributions, Estimation, Testing.
Vectors: Covered by GATE DA math + IISc probability modules. Track JAM official answer portals.

ISI Entrance 🌟🌟🌟🌟🌟 · Final Yr May

Eligibility: B.Tech/B.E. (MnC matches top criteria).
Syllabus: Advanced Probability, Combinatorics, Matrix Theory, Logic.
Vectors: ISI Kolkata master question brochures. Deep abstract analytical maths notes.

CMI Entrance 🌟🌟🌟🌟🌟 · Final Yr May

Eligibility: B.Tech with math-intensive computing tracks.
Syllabus: Proof-based Math, Abstract Algebra, TCS.
Vectors: CMI past papers. Proof-based classical algebra (Gallian, Dummit & Foote).

TIFR GS (Math/CS) 🌟🌟🌟🌟🌟 · Final Yr Dec

Eligibility: B.Tech/B.E. standard equivalent.
Syllabus: Logic puzzles, Discrete Math, Combinatorics, algorithmic efficiency.
Vectors: TIFR past GS papers. Deep mental reasoning, not formulas.

JEST (Theoretical CS) 🌟🌟🌟🌟 · Feb/Mar

Eligibility: B.Tech/M.Sc matching computing profiles.
Syllabus: Discrete Math, Automata, Graph Theory, DSA, algorithm complexities.
Vectors: Standard Discrete Math (Kenneth Rosen) solves this.

NBHM Scholarship 🌟🌟🌟🌟🌟 · Jan/Feb

Eligibility: 3rd/4th Yr B.Tech with strong math track.
Syllabus: Algebra (Group/Ring/Field), Real & Complex Analysis, Geometry.
Vectors: NBHM archive papers. Keep proofs clear on paper.

📊 B.Tech MnC — Programme Structure (128 Credits)

Core Courses: 50 Credits — Advanced Mathematics (Linear Algebra, Probability, Analysis) & Computing (DSA, Automata, AI/ML)

Breadth Soft Core: 14 Credits — Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Material Science, Earth Science

Humanities: 9 Credits — Mandatory humanities & social science courses

Soft Core: 21 Credits — Balance Maths (≥6cr) & Computing (≥6cr). Clash rules: E1 222/E2 202/MA 262 choose 1; E2 212/E0 298/MA 219 choose 1; E0 220/MA 216 choose 1

ISP/Project/Electives: 34 Credits — Sem 7-8: ISP (12cr max) + Open electives across IISc

Duration: 4 Years · 8 Semesters · Start: August · Bengaluru
